Privacy Policy

Ask Tony does not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

All app settings (cities, commute preferences, thresholds) are stored locally on your device.

The app uses Apple MapKit for city search and Open‑Meteo for weather data. No personal data is transmitted in these requests.

Ask Tony uses local notifications generated directly on your device.

Advertising

The app displays non-personalized advertisements using a third-party provider (e.g., Google AdMob).

  • No user tracking across apps or websites
  • No use of advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • No personalized advertising

Ads are based solely on general context.

The app does not store or transmit user data to external servers.
