About the App

Ask Tony helps riders decide whether the weather is good for riding. Just add your cities or route and Tony gives you a clear answer—ride or skip it.

Common Questions

How does Tony decide?
Tony looks at temperature and precipitation to determine if conditions are suitable for riding.

Why didn’t I get a notification?
Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for Ask Tony.

Can I change my commute times?
Yes—g o into settings to customize when Tony checks the weather.
